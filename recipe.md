Take oreo biscuits . 
Crush them finely.
Now keep it aside.
Take cold heavy cream.
Whip to stiff peaks.
Add sweetened condensed milk in it.
Beat on low speed until incorporated.
Now add the crushed biscuits and gently fold and mix properly.
Put it in an ice cream tray.
Top with 3-4 extra crushed biscuits.
Cover it with an plastic wrapper.
Freeze for atleast 6 hours.
The ice cream is ready!!!
